About FLIB

More about FLIB

Overview:

FLIB (Fundamental Lessons In Biology) is an innovative project designed to provide comprehensive biology lessons through online video content. The project leverages the expertise of students who have attended at least Camp 2 to deliver high-quality, engaging educational material.
