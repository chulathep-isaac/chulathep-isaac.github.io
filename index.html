About Me

Name: Chulathep Silapanuntakul

Chulathep Silapanuntakul ’16 is an upper secondary student from Bangkok, Thailand who studies at Suankularb Wittayalai School as a Science-Mathematics student being taught in the English language. At Suankularb, Chulathep is the incumbent President of Technology and Development Club, managing and promoting interest, awareness and research orienting towards the discipline for both inside and outside the organization. Prior to being promoted, he also hosted the club’s podcast. Moreover, he was the gold medalist for the Singapore-Thailand Science Immersion Programme 2023 hosted by the Faculty of Science, National University of Singapore. He is the founder and the organizer of the ToongRaoHaiYeum Project, a project that aims to provide donated paper andm fabric bags, reusing them for patients-in-need in rural and urban hospitals alike. Given his family's background in healthcare, particularly with his father's role as an Associate Professor in Public Health, Chulathep aspires to pursue a medical degree at the Faculty of Medicine, Siriraj Hospital, Mahidol University, Thailand upon graduation.
